Overview

  • What is music technology?
    • Music technology is any device, machine or tool used by musicians and composers to make, record, perform, compose, notate, analyze or edit music.
  • Music technology consists of software and hardware.
  • It falls into 3 catagories: mechanical, electric, and electronic/digital.

Mechanical music technology

Mechanical music technology is any physical device that is used to make music. Some examples include a flute, piano, trumpet and pipe.

Electric music technology

Electric music technology is any instrument that uses electrical circuits. Some examples include the electric guitar, electric piano, and the electric bass. Electronic music technology

Electronic music technology is any device that is used to produce music digitally. Some examples include a computer, and a digital audio workstation (DAW).
